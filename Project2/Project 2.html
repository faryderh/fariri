    1. GARAU-GARAU

      2. Garau Garau is a popular Northern Nigeria dish. it is commonly prepared by the Hausas, Fulani, and Hausa-Fulani people of Nigeria.
      It is easy to make and economy friendly. It is quite good a meal also.


      Meal Ingredients Preparation
      GARAU-GARAU Rice, Reans, Oil,
      Grinded pepper (Yagi),
      Lettuce, Onions,
      Tomatoes,Salt, Egg (optional).
      		 Boil your beans and rice,and egg till it's cooked.
      Heat the oil, fry the onioin.
      Some prefer the onion not fried, slice the onions, tomatoes, and lettuce.
      Serve your meal, add oil, pepper(Yagi), sliced onion, tomatoes, egg and lettuce.

    2. NIGERIAN FRIED STEW

      3. A mouth-watering tomato stew. This is the most common type of stew in Nigeria.
      It can be served with literaly every meal,be it Rice, Swallow, Porridge, Jollof Rice and Fried Rice.
      It is very delicious and the perfection would tingle everyone's taste buds.


      Meal Ingredients Preparation
      NIGERIAN FRIED STEW Chicken, Onions,
      Ball Peppers, Tomatoes,
      Seasoning, Vegetable oil
      Sachet Tomato paste, Salt.      		 If you are using chicken, fish, or meat, prepare it before starting the stew, I'll be using chicken.
      Grind your tomatoes,onions and ball pepper together.
      Pour in a pot, place on a cooker and leave till the water almost drys up, leaving you with a paste.
      Drop the paste from the cooker, place an empty pot on the cooker, pour oil and leave till hot.
      Pour a sachet tomato paste, and sliced onion, and other Seasoning you want to use, turn till it looks like a sauce
      Then pour your cooked tomato paste, turn and leave to fry.
      It is advisable to use a large pot so as to avoid the stew pouring away.
      When the stew is almost ready, add your chicken (the Chicken can be fried or cooked), turn again and close.
      When the stew is cooked you will know cause it start to give an aroma, but you can also taste it to be sure.
