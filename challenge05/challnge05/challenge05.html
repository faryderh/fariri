The coolest creature

The San Francisco garter snake Some people consider the San Francisco garter snake to be the most beautiful species in the world. That is, of course, subjective, but it's a stunningly beautiful snake. Known for the bright turquoise stripes that run alongside deep coral and black, the snake is a sight to behold. Unfortunately, it's also an exceedingly rare sight. This snake is on the Endangered Species list, with perhaps as few as 1,000-2,000 adults remaining on Earth. It's difficult to do the snake's coloration justice in a photo.
